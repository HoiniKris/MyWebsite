The news page

This is the first project and the first webpage that I have worked on during the courses at IT School where I have learned web development. For the navigation bar and carousel I have used the Bootstrap framework. I used object-fit to keep the ratio for the pictures and for the layout I have used Flexbox. What I liked about this project was the fact that I had the chance to make a webpage starting from nothing on it, and the oportunity to play with the proprietis of Css.
